# CURSED 
js13k entry 2019

*****
You, a shapeshifting cursor, were cursed, losing your ability to change shape and 
cast forward through browser windows. Find your shapes as you travel back to your home. 

## Controls
- W - up
- A - left
- S - down
- D - right
- Space - fire
- Cursor - aim
